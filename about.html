About EduQuiz Explorer

Your personalized roadmap & quiz platform 🚀

What is EduQuiz Explorer?

EduQuiz Explorer is a smart platform that helps students explore different fields like Machine Learning, Web Development, Cyber Security, and more. It provides structured roadmaps and interactive quizzes to guide users step-by-step in their learning journey.

Why This Platform?

Many students feel confused about where to start learning a new skill. DreamFinder solves this problem by giving clear learning paths and quick quizzes to test knowledge instantly.

Key Features

📚 Structured Roadmaps
🧠 Interactive Quizzes
⚡ Smart Search
🚀 Fast & Responsive UI

How It Works

1. Search or choose a field

2. View roadmap with structured steps

3. Take quizzes to test your knowledge
